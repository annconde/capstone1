"My Puhunan" features Vie Reyes, the woman behind 18 Days Coffee Roaster and Bote Central Inc. She looks back on the difficulties she encountered when she first started her business, and explains the steps in making coffee. Vie then discusses the importance of coming up with a sustainable business model that would benefit coffee farmers and small cooperatives. Jojo Andal and Arnel Gonzales share their experiences as coffee farmers, and encourage the youth to learn more about coffee farming. Finally, Vie talks about the sense of fulfillment that she gets from her work in the coffee industry.
